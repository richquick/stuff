Answers to common questions

If you have other questions just talk to our team, support@gathercontent.com

What is a collection?

A collection is a group of pages which could be a project, or on-going content initiative. For example, a website re-design, or on-going blog content.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes. You get 2 months for free when you sign-up for a year.

Can I be invoiced instead?

Invoices can only be sent when paying for a year in-advance. Get in touch with our support team and we will get everything arranged for you.

Is all of my content safe and secure?

Yes, we take our backup and infrastructure our very seriously. We run hourly backups of our databases, and have full server redundancy. You can also export your content at any time.

What is VAT?

All prices exclude VAT. If you are located in the EU the prevailing UK rate (20%) will be applied to your bill.

Do I have to sign a contract?

No. Our service is pay-as-you-go. If you cancel your account will remain active until the end of your billing period then be made in-active until you wish to re-subscribe. You can always completely delete your account too.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel from within your account settings.

What types of payment do you accept?

Currently we accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We can accept Paypal for yearly subscriptions.

Questions?

Pop our support team an email and we'll get back to you as soon as we can (usually within a couple of hours).
