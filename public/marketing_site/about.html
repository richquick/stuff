About GatherContent

We're building a tool agencies can use with their clients to produce meaningful, intelligent, robust and future-friendly structured content.

We believe in taking a structured approach to content before the CMS, or any publishing platform. Managing content production with Word documents isn't particularly manageable, accessible, logical or fun, and neither is producing content in a CMS.

GatherContent could be called a pre-CMS, or better, output-agnostic. It allows people to create structured content early and publish it anywhere. Our API allows content to be adapted and channeled to multiple outputs, or publishing platforms.

The idea is that the creation of intelligent content is becoming a lot more important than the publishing of content in general.

When we lose the ability to control where content will end up, travel to, or how it will be consumed; we need to focus less on predicting the output or dictating the display, and more on what we are saying and what we want the content do. What is the best way to structure and construct this message or achieve this function? How can we give information the best chance of working in the chaos?

GatherContent is a small team of seven people working together to create a better way of working on digital content. Originally based in the north east of Scotland we're now working from the lively Brick Lane, London.

Inspired by, and sick of, the chaos that so often reigns when working with clients to pull together the content of web projects, we've spent the last three years building an online tool to help people gather their content.

It's great to be able to say that we now help over 7000 companies in 133 countries work on content with their team and clients.

We're hiring smart people to help us build the future of content collaboration.
